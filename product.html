Discover the Delight of Ube Cheese Tikoy Roll!

The Ube Cheese Tikoy Roll offers a unique twist on the traditional tikoy and is a delicious combination of traditional Filipino and Chinese flavors. The tikoy is sliced into strips and paired with cheese, then wrapped in spring roll wrappers. This treat features a vibrant purple color due to the ube flavoring, making it stand out from the usual brown or white tikoy. It has a smooth, slightly glossy surface before cooking, which becomes crispy when fried.

Unlike the classic sweet and plain tikoy, this version has a mild, nut-like flavor, with hints of vanilla from the ube. It’s typically served warm, so the cheese stays melted and sticky. The difference in textures—crispy on the outside, chewy on the inside, and gooey cheese—makes it unique. Perfect for celebrations or as an everyday merienda.
