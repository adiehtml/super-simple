Discover the Delight of Ube Cheese Tikoy Roll!

The Ube Cheese Tikoy Roll is a unique twist on traditional Filipino Tikoy. Wrapped in a crispy lumpia wrapper and filled with a combination of ube and cheese, this snack offers the perfect balance of crispy, chewy, and gooey goodness.

With its vibrant purple color, rich ube flavor, and creamy cheese filling, the Ube Tikoy Roll is a delightful and visually stunning snack. Enjoy it warm, and let the cheese melt in your mouth while the outside stays crispy!
